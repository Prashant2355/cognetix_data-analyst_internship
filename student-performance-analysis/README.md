## Objective :-

## Analysis & Visualizations :-

The following key analyses were performed:

- Distribution of **Math Scores**
- Average scores by **Gender**
- Impact of **Parental Education** on student performance
- Performance comparison across **Race/Ethnicity groups**
- Effect of **Test Preparation Course** on scores

Professional charts such as **histograms, bar charts, box plots, and line plots** were used to clearly communicate insights.

---

## Key Insights :-

- Students with higher parental education levels generally perform better.
- Female students outperform males in reading and writing.
- Math scores show wider variability compared to other subjects.
- Race/Ethnicity Group E consistently achieves higher average scores.
- Test preparation courses have a positive impact on student performance.
